Small portions

small portions

Feed the fish in small portions. While many people hear that fish require a tiny amount of chips per eating, giving the fish a large amount of food causes digestion problems or makes the tank dirty and unhealthy.

Type of food: Whatever type of dry food is used, just put the amount of food the fish may eat for 3-5 minutes. If you place a large amount of food in the sink, remove it using a thin mesh.

The place of the basin

THe are a set of steps and rules that must be taken into account when choosing the place where the aquarium will be placed, so that its location is suitable for the living conditions that fish need, such as the aquarium near the source of the water, so that it can be filled easily and quickly, in addition to the need to keep it away from sunlight , Reaching the sun is enough for fish for only an hour .
